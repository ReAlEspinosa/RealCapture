My love for photography started when I was in grade school and was so eager and fascinated to browse the new edition of Nat Geo Magazine sent to my grandfather. Situated on a remote island in the Philippines, holding a good camera is my greatest wish. Years passed and life dictated my journey and led me to be an IT professional. I never thought of owning a good camera again until a friend let me use his camera on a summer trip to Thailand. From then, I never stopped capturing faces, places, and moments.
